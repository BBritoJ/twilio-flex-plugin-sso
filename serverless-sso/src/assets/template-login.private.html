Serverless SSO Flex Plugin

Login into Twilio Flex

This is a simple Flex Plugin to show how easy it is to have Flex working without a SaaS Identity Provider (IdP) of the market. You can build it.

The benefits of this plugin are in general for small/mid sized companies who do not have an IdP and, therefore, having Flex running quickly can be a barrier - but not anymore :-)

Also, with a little extension of the Code, you can extend the validation part to access your internal databases to verify if that user is a known Agent to access your Flex instance. The possibilities of authentication are endless.

Oh, I almost forgot: As always, this is not production-ready. Your Developers have to look into the code and sign off - but we are here to help you in any doubts you might have!
